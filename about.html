About Us

We are a local small business focused on the fashion industry. With strong relationships and knowledge of quality recognition, we promise to offer a selection of clothing for men and women within the thrift world. Thrifting may still be unfamiliar to many people, but we believe that through hard work and maintaining high quality, something great will be achieved in the future.
